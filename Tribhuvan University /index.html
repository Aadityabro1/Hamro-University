About Tribhuvan University

Established in 1959, TU is Nepal's first national institution of higher education. With 62 constituent campuses and over 1,080 affiliated colleges, it serves as the cornerstone of Nepal's educational system.

Academic Excellence

TU offers over 1,000 undergraduate and 500 postgraduate programs across diverse disciplines. Ranked among Asia's top universities, it continues to produce skilled professionals for Nepal and beyond.
